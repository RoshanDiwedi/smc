You can become a Sub Broker in 5 easy steps:

• Drop the enquiry

• Receive the call from our help desk

• Provide all documents as stated in the checklist

• Submit the cheque along with registration amount

• Your code is generated

You need the following documents (for Individual) to open an account:

• Pan Card

• Aadhar Card

• Educational proof (minimum intermediate)

• Residential address proof

• Office address proof (electricity bill, phone bill-BSNL or MTNL, bank statement)

• Four photographs

• CA reference letter

• Name affidavit in case of any kind of mismatch in name on a Rs. 10 stamp paper

Note:

Above documents should be attested from a Notary or CA
